Historical Art

Art has existed almost as long as humankind: from early prehistoric art to contemporary art. The early sense of the definition of art comes from the Latin meaning, which translates to "skill" or "craft" as you would assosiate with words like "artisan". Art is important to history as it reflects the values and culture. By looking at a work of art and its symbolism, colors, materials, etc. we can learn more about the culture that produced it.
