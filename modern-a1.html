TED WARNELL

Ted Warnell is a visual artist and writer specializing in creative new media. His work is found in digital and physical art galleries. Warnell is Poetry Editor for The BeeHive, a journal of hypertext and hypermedia work, and publisher/editor of a Room without Walls, his own online site of visual, literary, and new media arts, and for the last few years, he has been producing visual poetry using HTML.
