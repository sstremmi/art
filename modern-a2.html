JENNY ODELL

Jenny Odell is an American artist, writer, and educator from Oakland, California. She has taught Internet art and digital/physical design at Stanford University since 2013. Her book,"How to Do Nothing: Resisting the Attention Economy" became a New York Times Best Seller in 2019 . Odell's work consists of acts of close observations such as bird watching, collecting screenshots, or trying to parse bizarre forms of e-commerce. Many of her artistic projects re-use existing objects or images and put them in context, for example images from Google Earth.
