AUGUSTE RODIN

François Auguste René Rodin was a French sculptor, generally considered the founder of modern sculpture. He was deeply inspired by tradition, yet tended to reject it's ideals. He believed that art should be true to nature and that philosophy shaped his attitude to materials and models, and therefore his art. Over his artisitc career, Rodin developed a way of displaying physical states that coorespond to expressions of turmoil or overwhelming joy. Because of this, few people who recognize Rodin's sculptures fail to be moved by them.
